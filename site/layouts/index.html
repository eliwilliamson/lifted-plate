Our Story

We are a family-owned and -operated business based in Oakland, CA.

We started as owners of an assisted living home in Walnut Creek. This home had been in our family for generations, and had a reputation for its excellent food. We expanded this tradition to include special events – which we styled, managed, and catered to perfection. It wasn’t long before our clients were asking us to plan and cater their own events.

With demand for our services increasing, we made the decision to go full time. And so Lifted Plate was born – out of a tradition of excellent service, superior ingredients, sustainable business practices, and impeccable attention to detail.

For us, there’s nothing more satisfying than watching an event come to life – complete with laughter, new friends, and full bellies. We truly love what we do. We hope to share our passion and experience with you – to make your event the best and most memorable it can be.
